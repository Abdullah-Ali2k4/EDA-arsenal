## Problem Understanding

**Dataset:** Titanic  
**Goal:** Predict whether a passenger survived the sinking of Titanic.  
**Target:** Survived (0 = not survived, 1 = survived)  
**Problem type:** Binary Classification

### Business question
Which passengers were more likely to survive the Titanic disaster?

### Evaluation metrics (possible)
- Accuracy
- F1-score
- Recall (important if missing survivors is costly)
